JavaScript does not have typed _variables_ - instead it has typed _values_ such 
as: `string`, `number`, `boolean`, `object`, `null` and `undefined`.

We will play about with `string`, `number` and `boolean` here and will go into 
more details about `object`, `null` and `undefined` later.
